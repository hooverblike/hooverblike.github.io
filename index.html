Welcome to our Bed & Breakfast!

We offer a nice and comfortable enviorment for you and your party to relax at. With lavish and comforting rooms to yummy and fresh food, you will have a great time staying at our spot!

Plus, we occasionally have events that occur during the weekend, so don't forget to check that out a head of time if you are wanting to do something besides resting and eating breakfest while your here!

Our breakfast selection

Occasionally for breakfast, you would have a set choice from different eggs, pancakes, and other breakfast items. Want boiled eggs, or perhaps sunnyside eggs, or maybe an omlet? We have that!

All you would need to do is fill out a form, or call our front desk, to put in your breakfast order. If there is something that isn't on our menu you that you would like, feel free to ask our front desk, or contact us ahead of time.
